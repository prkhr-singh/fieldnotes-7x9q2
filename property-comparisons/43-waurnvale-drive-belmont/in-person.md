**Kitchen:** Outstanding. "Kitchen bahut accha hai" — the strongest single-room reaction of the day. New kitchen as part of the recent renovation program. One buyer later regretted not inspecting it even more thoroughly. This is the standout room.

**Main bathroom:** New as part of the recent renovation. However, the first impression was that it felt a bit small. The secondary bathroom was preferred over the master bathroom.

**Ensuite:** Updated earlier — predates the current owners' renovation program and was left as-is because it was already done. Means the ensuite is from the pre-2022 era; worth checking in B&P for waterproofing behind older tiles.

**Secondary bathroom:** Preferred over the master — felt more generous in scale.

**Bedrooms:** Split systems installed in all 3 bedrooms as part of the recent renovation. Brand new units. Heating and cooling available throughout via the split systems plus the main living unit.

**Main living:** Split system present. Large windows were immediately called out as a positive — natural light was one of the most repeated praise points through the entire inspection.

**Garage:** Flooring noted positively. Gate arrangement allows vehicle to drive through if desired — practical flexibility.

**Outdoor / yard:** Gates allow the yard to be sectioned off into separate zones. Boundary looked enclosed around the property. Compact footprint — buyers saw the home as tidy and manageable rather than sprawling. The sloped/hilly character of the land takes up a noticeable part of the block — the usable flat area may be less than the 641m² total implies.

**Natural light:** Repeated as one of the inspection's strongest positives. Large windows compensate for any orientation concern. Light was judged positively even on a darker inspection day — a meaningful signal.

**Character:** One buyer noted a "US house design" feel — a sense of distinctive quality and considered layout that separated this from generic stock. This is the intangible that the pre-visit Barry Plant media did not convey.
