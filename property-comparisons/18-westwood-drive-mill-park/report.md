## Quick Verdict

18 Westwood is interesting because it gives strong internal density on a very compact 241m2 lot: four bedrooms, two bathrooms, double garage, balcony/courtyard and a low-maintenance outdoor profile. On pure daily upkeep, it fits better than the larger older Mill Park blocks.

However, the listing calls it a townhouse, and that conflicts with your strong preference for freestanding house / no body corporate / no shared-title complexity. Treat this as conditionally rejected until title, owners corporation and shared-wall position are verified.

## Photo Read

The interior is bright and usable, but not new. Kitchen and bathrooms look older but serviceable. The master and some upstairs bedrooms feel spacious in the photos. The courtyard/balcony setup aligns well with your low-maintenance preference.

The external presentation reads townhouse/terrace-like, and that is the biggest issue.

## Price Lens

If this were a clean freestanding home, the low-maintenance internal density would be attractive in the high $700s. Because of title/type risk, it needs a discount. Do not let the lower guide compensate for a property format you do not actually want.
