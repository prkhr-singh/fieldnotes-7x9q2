## All-Electric Rule (Important)

New state government rule: any new build **starting in 2027 or later** will need to be all-electric. Key implications:

- Stage 13 (settle mid-June 2026): may still allow gas/electric choice if construction starts this year
- Stage 12 (November 2026 settlement): if building starts in 2027, realistically all-electric
- Affected appliances: cooktops, ovens — likely electric
- Heating: gas ducted heating uncertain; alternative heating systems may be required
- Rep was unsure whether exemptions or fees could preserve gas usage
- Build costs expected to rise slightly due to the rule change

**Practical implication:** If you care about gas cooking, Stage 13 (settle mid-June) is the only realistic window. Stage 12 and beyond → plan for all-electric kitchen and heating.

## Stage Recommendation (for these buyers)

**Stage 12 is the rep's recommendation** for buyers prioritising location over speed. Deposit now secures the block; November registration gives time to explore builders and finalise plans. Construction cannot begin until settlement.

**Stage 10/11 waterway lots** are the aspirational target — better position, parkland/waterway outlook, likely Stage 10 or 11 release (a few months away for Stage 10). The waiting period allows proper builder research without rushed decisions.
