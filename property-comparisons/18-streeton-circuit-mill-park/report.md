## Quick Verdict

18 Streeton is a practical older single-level 4-bed / 2-bath Mill Park house on about 603m2. The fourth bedroom improves the work-from-home story compared with a basic 3-bed, and the kitchen/family/living areas are functional.

It is not a dream fit. The rooms are more modest, the house reads older, and the backyard is much larger than your preferred low-maintenance outdoor style. It is most useful as a value benchmark: good if the price stays controlled, weak if it approaches better-fit homes.
