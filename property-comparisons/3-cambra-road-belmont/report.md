## Photo And Floorplan Read

The floorplan is materially better than a typical 3-bed/no-study house. The separate home office is the key differentiator: unlike 36 Littlewood or 8 Northam, WFH does not consume a bedroom. Room spaciousness is strong: the master and one secondary bedroom are both large, and the smaller 3.6 x 3.0m bedroom is still usable.

The outdoor/garden is a double-edged positive. It gives character, usable deck space and a better lifestyle feel than a new estate courtyard, but it also adds maintenance and drainage/tree-root risk.

## Orientation And Light
