## Photo And Floorplan Read

Photos show a polished, character-leaning townhouse: timber floors, neutral walls, quality kitchen, light living area, generous bathroom, private deck, and a large upstairs master. The main bedroom is clearly the strongest lifestyle space. The ground-floor layout is unusually practical for this property type because the two secondary bedrooms share the main bathroom and separate WC, and the garage has internal access plus a storeroom.

The floorplan also confirms the compromise. The garage and driveway dominate the narrow frontage, the outdoor area is a courtyard/deck rather than land, and the third bedroom is the only realistic WFH space. The home is probably pleasant, but it is not aligned with the freestanding-house search.

## Price Lens

The $765k-$795k guide is not obviously cheap for the brief because the property is being compared against freestanding houses and stronger 4-bed/retreat layouts, not only Geelong townhouse stock. A nearby same-street/area townhouse comp, 9A Mary Street, reportedly sold for $750k in December 2025; that supports the guide, but it does not solve the hard-filter issue.

For this household, the only rational reconsideration would be a major discount or an explicit decision to permit own-title townhouses. Without that change, even a fair market price is not a good purchase fit.
