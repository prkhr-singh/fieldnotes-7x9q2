### The 8 Questions — Ask at Every Display Home

| # | Question | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | "What is the all-in price to build exactly what I'm standing in right now, on a standard flat lot?" | Plaque price is the base. What you see is 20–40% more. |
| 2 | "What's included in the base price, and what in this display is an upgrade?" | Get the upgrade price list — flooring, stone benchtops, alfresco, facade, fencing, driveway, landscaping. |
| 3 | "What land do you have available nearby and at what price? Total H&L all-in?" | Land + build = the real number. |
| 4 | "What is the realistic timeline from signing a land contract to keys?" | Get: land registration wait + pre-site (permits, selections) + construction. Honest answer is 18–24 months from today. |
| 5 | "Is this a fixed-price contract? What happens if material or trade costs change during construction?" | Fixed price = safer. Cost-plus = your risk. |
| 6 | "Are you a participating lender under the First Home Buyer Guarantee scheme? And is the land priced under $750k for FHOG?" | FHBG requires builder to be approved. Regional VIC FHOG is $20,000 (not $10k) for new builds under $750k total. |
| 7 | "What is your current wait time for a build commencement slot?" | Some builders are 3 months out, some are 12. |
| 8 | "If I'm renting while I build, what carrying cost should I plan for over the construction period?" | You already know the answer (~$24–45k at $2,000–2,500/month), but their response reveals how straight they are with you. |

---

### Financial Context to Keep in Mind

- **Stamp duty saving on H&L:** Pay duty on land only (~$350k land → ~$18k duty) vs established ($850k → ~$46k). Saving: ~$28k.
- **But carrying cost:** 18–24 months rent at $2,000–2,500/month = $36–60k. Saving is largely absorbed.
- **Regional FHOG:** $20,000 for new builds in Geelong LGA under $750k total. Higher than metro $10k.
- **Wife's commute constraint:** Armstrong Creek to Fitzroy/Bundoora by V/Line is ~90 min each way. This constraint doesn't change regardless of how good the display home feels.
- **H&L is deprioritised — this visit is for calibration**, not a buying decision.
