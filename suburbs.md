## Why Development Happens Where It Does (for H&L context)

- **Urban Growth Boundary (UGB):** The legal line determining where Melbourne can grow. Nothing outside it can be developed residentially.
- **5 growth corridors:** North (Hume), North-West (Melton), West (Wyndham), South-East (Casey/Cardinia), Geelong.
- **PSP process:** VPA prepares Precinct Structure Plans before any lot can be sold. Takes 3–7 years.

**Next wave areas (titles/activity likely 2026–2032):**
- Beveridge/Wallan (Hume, north of Kalkallo) — cheapest Hume land, future train station proposed
- Sunbury South — PSPs active, ~45 min, underrated
- Donnybrook/Woodstock — active now, between Craigieburn and Beveridge
